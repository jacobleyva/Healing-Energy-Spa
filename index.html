~ ABOUT OUR SPA ~

Here at Healing Energy Spa our goal is to provide therapeutic massages to fit each clients personal needs. With over 15 years of experience in massage therapy and our wide array of personalized services we aim to; reduce pain, relieve stress, eliminate fatigue, improve range of motion, increase circulation, and balance & center energy.
